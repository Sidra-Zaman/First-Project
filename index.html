Our Services

Cardiologist

Our Cardiology Department provides advanced diagnostic services like ECG, echocardiography, and stress testing, along with treatments. Combining cutting-edge technology with personalized care, we are dedicated to improving heart health and enhancing quality of life for our patients.

Nephrologist

Our Nephrology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating kidney-related conditions, including chronic kidney disease, kidney stones, and hypertension. With advanced diagnostic tools and personalized care, we aim to preserve kidney function and enhance overall health.

Neurologist

Our Neurology Department focuses on diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. From migraines and epilepsy to stroke and neurodegenerative diseases, we provide expert care using advanced technology and personalized treatment plans.
